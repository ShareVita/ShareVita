Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

ShareVita ("we," "us," or "our") is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN 39-4771264) based in Oakland, California. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on sharevita.org. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. What data we collect

sharevita.org does not require you to create an account or log in. The only personal information we may receive is what you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your email address (optional)
  • The message you write

This information is sent directly to our team via email. We do not store it in a database or any third-party service on our end.

2. Analytics

No analytics or tracking services are currently configured on sharevita.org. We do not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any other analytics platform on this site. We do not collect data about your browsing behavior, page views, or time on site.

3. Cookies

sharevita.org does not set any cookies. We have no advertising cookies, tracking cookies, session cookies, or persistent cookies of any kind on this website.

4. How your information is used

If you contact us through our contact form or directly by email, we use your information solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not:

  • Sell your information to any third party
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Add you to a mailing list without your explicit consent
  • Use your contact information for any purpose other than responding to you

5. Data retention

Inquiries submitted via email are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to and resolve your request. We do not maintain long-term databases of contact submissions.

6. Your rights

You have the right to request deletion of any personal information you have shared with us. To make a deletion request, email us at team@sharevita.org with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. External links

sharevita.org links to external websites and platforms including shareskippy.com and ridesharetahoe.com. These are separate platforms with their own privacy policies and data practices. ShareVita is not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review their policies when visiting those platforms.

8. Contact for privacy questions

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how ShareVita handles personal information, please contact us at team@sharevita.org.
