ShareSkippy

ShareSkippy is a free community platform that connects dog owners with neighbors who want to borrow dogs for walks, hikes, and adventures ‚Äî without the full-time commitment of ownership. Designed for city dwellers, renters, and dog lovers who can't have their own pet, ShareSkippy works through simple profile matching and community trust. Dogs get more exercise and social time, owners get free reliable help, and communities grow stronger through shared care and real connection.

RideShareTahoe

RideShareTahoe is a free community carpool platform connecting Bay Area residents headed to Lake Tahoe's ski resorts, trails, and shorelines. Drivers post available seats; riders request to join ‚Äî no booking fees, no algorithms, just neighbors coordinating trips together. Every shared ride reduces traffic on I-80 and Highway 50, lowers carbon emissions, and makes Tahoe more accessible to people without cars or on limited budgets.

About ShareVita

Our mission

"ShareVita operates on the belief that technology should bring people closer together, not further apart."

ShareVita is a community-driven nonprofit helping people help each other again. We use technology with purpose and compassion to rebuild neighborhoods and belonging. Our platforms make it easier to share time, skills, and care ‚Äî reconnecting communities through real-world support and digital tools that feel human.

ShareVita has received IRS determination as a 501(c)(3) public charity. All programs and platforms are free to use. We do not sell user data or charge fees for community participation. Donations and volunteers help us grow and build more tools.
