About ShareVita

"ShareVita operates on the belief that technology should bring people closer together, not further apart."

ShareVita is a community-driven nonprofit helping people help each other again. We use technology with purpose and compassion to rebuild neighborhoods and belonging. Our platforms make it easier to share time, skills, and care — reconnecting communities through real-world support and digital tools that feel human.

Our Projects

ShareSkippy

A free community app that connects dog owners and dog lovers for walks, cuddles, and companionship — built around kindness, not transactions.

RideShareTahoe

A community carpool platform connecting Bay Area and Tahoe travelers — reducing environmental impact while making weekend adventures more social and affordable.
