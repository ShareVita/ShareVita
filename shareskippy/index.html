Getting started

See How It Works

Five simple steps to your first dog adventure.

1

Create your profile

Sign up and set your location, role (owner, dog lover, or both), and what you're looking for.

2

Post or browse

Owners post when their dog is available. Dog lovers post when they're free to help. Both sides browse by location.

3

Send a message

Find someone who's a good fit and send a message to introduce yourself and discuss the arrangement.

4

Schedule a meeting

Request a meeting with a specific date, time, and public location ‚Äî all arranged through the platform.

5

Leave a review

After your meetup, leave a star rating and written feedback to help build a trusted, safe community.
