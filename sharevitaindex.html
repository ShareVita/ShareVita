About ShareVita

ShareVita operates on the belief that technology should bring people closer together, not further apart.

We’re a community-driven nonprofit with one goal — to help people help each other again. While technology has connected the world, it has also contributed to a growing loneliness epidemic. We believe that when technology is used with purpose and compassion, it can rebuild neighborhood and belonging.

Our mission is to reconnect communities by creating digital platforms that make it easy for neighbors to share time, skills, and care. Whether it’s helping an elderly neighbor walk their dog or sharing a ride to the mountains, ShareVita brings people together through small acts of kindness that make a big difference.

We’re starting small — one project, one connection at a time — but our vision is big: a world where technology strengthens human connection instead of replacing it.
